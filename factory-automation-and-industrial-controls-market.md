The industrial controls market is categorized into field devices and industrial control systems. Field devices include sensors, actuators, and transmitters that gather data and execute physical actions. Industrial control systems encompass software and hardware that manage and direct manufacturing operations, such as PLCs, DCS, and SCADA systems. Together, these components streamline production, improve safety, and facilitate real-time monitoring and control of industrial processes.</p></p>
