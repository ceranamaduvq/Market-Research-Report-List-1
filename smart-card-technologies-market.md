<p><ul><li>Contact Card</li><li>Contactless Card</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smart card technologies market is primarily divided into two types: contact cards and contactless cards. Contact cards require physical connection to a reader, typically through a chip interface, allowing secure transactions and data exchange. In contrast, contactless cards utilize RFID or NFC technology, enabling transactions without physical contact, enhancing convenience and speed. Both types are widely used in various sectors, including finance, transportation, and access control, catering to the growing demand for secure and efficient identification solutions.</p></p>
