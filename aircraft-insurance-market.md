<p><strong>The Aircraft Insurance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Public Liability Insurance</li><li>Passenger Liability Insurance</li><li>Combined Single Limit (CSL)</li><li>Ground Risk Hull (Motion) Insurance</li><li>Ground Risk Hull (Non-Motion) Insurance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aircraft insurance encompasses various market types tailored to specific risks. Public Liability Insurance covers damages to third parties during flight operations. Passenger Liability Insurance protects against claims from injured or deceased passengers. Combined Single Limit (CSL) offers a unified coverage limit for both passenger and third-party liabilities. Ground Risk Hull (Motion) Insurance insures aircraft while taxiing, taking off, or landing, while Ground Risk Hull (Non-Motion) Insurance covers damage when the aircraft is stationary on the ground.</p></p>
