<p><p>The NDT equipment market includes various testing methods essential for inspecting materials and structures without causing damage. Magnetic Particle Testing detects surface and near-surface flaws in ferromagnetic materials, while Visual Testing employs the inspector's vision to assess components. Liquid Penetrant Testing reveals surface defects using dye, and Radiographic Testing utilizes X-rays or gamma rays for internal examination. Ultrasonic Testing employs sound waves to identify flaws within materials. Other methods encompass advanced technologies and specialized techniques, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
