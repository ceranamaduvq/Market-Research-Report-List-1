USB-connected printers offer direct and reliable connections to computers but limit mobility. Wi-Fi printers enable wireless printing, allowing users to connect from multiple devices across a network, enhancing convenience. USB/Wi-Fi models combine both technologies, providing flexibility for users who may need a wired connection or prefer the ease of wireless printing. Each type caters to diverse user preferences and environments.</p></p>
