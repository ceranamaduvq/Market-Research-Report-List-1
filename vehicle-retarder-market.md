<p><ul><li>Electric retarders</li><li>Hydraulic retarder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle retarder market comprises two primary types: electric and hydraulic retarders. Electric retarders utilize electromagnetic principles to create resistance, effectively slowing down the vehicle without significant wear on the braking system, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. Hydraulic retarders, on the other hand, use hydraulic fluid dynamics to dissipate kinetic energy, offering reliable performance in various terrains. Both types enhance vehicle safety and control, providing effective alternatives to traditional braking systems, particularly for commercial vehicles in demanding environments.</p></p>
