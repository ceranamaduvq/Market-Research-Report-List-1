<p><p>PERC (Passivated Emitter and Rear Cell) solar cells have two primary market types: single-side and double-side. Single-side PERC cells have enhanced efficiency through rear-side passivation, optimizing light capture from the front. In contrast, double-side PERC cells utilize both sides to capture sunlight, increasing energy yield by harnessing reflected light from surfaces beneath. This dual capability provides advantages in various installations, especially in space-constrained areas, contributing to a stronger market presence as efficiency demands grow in the solar industry.</p></p>
