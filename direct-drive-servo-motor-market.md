<p><p>The Direct Drive Servo Motor market encompasses two primary types: linear motors and rotary motors. Linear motors convert electrical energy directly into linear motion, making them ideal for applications requiring precise, high-speed movements along a straight path, such as CNC machining and conveyor systems. Conversely, rotary motors provide rotational motion, suitable for applications like robotics, automation, and industrial machinery. Both types enhance performance and efficiency by eliminating mechanical components, thereby reducing wear and maintenance while improving accuracy and responsiveness in various industrial contexts.</p></p>
