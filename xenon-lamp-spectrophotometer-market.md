<p><p>The Xenon Lamp Spectrophotometer market encompasses various types of instruments, each serving distinct analytical purposes. UV-Visible Spectrophotometers evaluate how substances absorb ultraviolet and visible light, crucial for concentration measurements. Infrared Spectrophotometers study molecular vibrations through infrared light absorption, facilitating organic compound identification. Lastly, Visible Spectrophotometers focus on the visible light spectrum for analyzing color and concentration in solutions.</p></p>
