<p><p>The non-reactive adhesives market comprises various types tailored for specific applications. Water-based adhesives utilize water as a solvent, offering low toxicity and easy cleanup, ideal for packaging and woodworking. Solvent-based adhesives contain organic solvents, delivering strong bonds suitable for more industrial applications, though they pose health risks. Hot melt adhesives are thermoplastic materials that provide quick bonding upon cooling, commonly used in packaging. Pressure-sensitive adhesives adhere upon contact and are widely used in labels and tapes, allowing for easy repositioning.</p></p>
