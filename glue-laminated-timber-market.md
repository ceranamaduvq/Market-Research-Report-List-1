<p><p>The Glue-Laminated Timber market is segmented into two primary types: Monolayer and Multi-storey Glue-Laminated Timber. Monolayer Glue-Laminated Timber consists of a single layer of laminated timber, offering versatility for various structural applications, particularly in smaller projects.
