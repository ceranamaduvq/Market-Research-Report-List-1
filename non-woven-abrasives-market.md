<p><p>Non-woven abrasives are engineered materials used in various surface finishing applications, characterized by their unique structure and flexibility. The market includes types like PA6 (Polyamide 6), PA66 (Polyamide 66), and PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate). PA6 offers good wear resistance and flexibility, making it suitable for light polishing tasks. PA66, with superior strength and thermal stability, is ideal for heavy-duty applications. PET, known for its durability and resistance to chemicals, is utilized in more rigorous applications, ensuring effective surface preparation and finishing.</p></p>
