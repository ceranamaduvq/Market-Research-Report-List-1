<p><p>The medical ultrasonic probe market includes various types catering to different diagnostic needs. Linear type probes offer high-resolution imaging for superficial structures, ideal for vascular and musculoskeletal exams. Convex type probes provide a wider field of view, suitable for abdominal and obstetric imaging. Phased array probes are compact, allowing for cardiac imaging with real-time data. Endocavitary probes are designed for internal examinations, such as transvaginal or transrectal scans, enhancing visualization of pelvic organs. Each type plays a crucial role in diverse medical applications.</p></p>
