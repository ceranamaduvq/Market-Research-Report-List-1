<p><p>Carbon Capture and Sequestration (CCS) involves various market types tailored to different industrial applications. </p><p>Industrial Process CCS captures CO2 directly from processes like cement and steel production. Oxy-combustion involves burning fossil fuels in pure oxygen, resulting in a concentrated CO2 stream. Pre-combustion CCS removes CO2 before combustion, often used in gasification processes. Post-combustion captures CO2 after fuel combustion, applicable to existing power plants. Each approach offers unique advantages for reducing greenhouse gas emissions across different sectors.</p></p>
