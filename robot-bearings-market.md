<p><p>The robot bearings market features various types tailored for specific applications. Deep groove ball bearings provide high load capacity and reliability, making them suitable for general robotic systems. Four point contact bearings offer efficient load handling in compact designs, ideal for arm joints. Angular contact bearings enable greater axial loads, enhancing precision in high-speed robots. Crossed roller bearings facilitate smooth motion in limited space, making them perfect for compact robots where stability and accuracy are crucial.</p></p>
