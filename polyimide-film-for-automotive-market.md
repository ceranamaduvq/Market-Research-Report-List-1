<p><strong>The Polyimide Film for Automotive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chemical Imidization Method</li><li>Thermal Imidization Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyimide film for the automotive market is categorized into two primary types based on production methods: chemical imidization and thermal imidization. The chemical imidization method involves a solution-based process where polyamic acids are converted into polyimides through chemical reactions, providing flexibility and ease of processing. In contrast, the thermal imidization method employs heat to achieve the same conversion, resulting in films that often possess superior thermal stability and mechanical properties, making them suitable for demanding automotive applications.</p></p>
