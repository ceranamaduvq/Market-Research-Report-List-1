<p><p>Optical polarimeters are devices used to measure the polarization of light, and the market can be categorized into single wavelength and double wavelength types. Single wavelength polarimeters operate at a fixed wavelength, providing specific information about polarimetry in a controlled environment. In contrast, double wavelength polarimeters utilize two distinct wavelengths, allowing for a broader range of measurements and enhanced sensitivity to changes in sample properties. This differentiation caters to various applications in research, pharmaceuticals, and optical material analysis.</p></p>
