<p><p>The vertical farming lighting market primarily includes halogen and LED types. Halogen lights offer high-intensity illumination and a broad spectrum, but are less energy-efficient and generate significant heat, requiring additional cooling. In contrast, LED lights are energy-efficient, long-lasting, and emit specific light spectrums tailored for plant growth. LEDs produce minimal heat, enhancing climate control in vertical farms. As sustainability and energy costs become critical, the LED segment is increasingly favored for modern vertical farming applications.</p></p>
