<p><strong>The Ultrapure Water Purification Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reverse Osmosis</li><li>EDI</li><li>Ion Exchange</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrapure water purification systems are essential for industries requiring high-purity water. The market includes three main technologies: </p><p>1. **Reverse Osmosis (RO)**: This process uses semi-permeable membranes to remove contaminants and ions, producing high-quality water. </p><p>2. **Electrodeionization (EDI)**: Combining ion exchange and electrochemical processes, EDI continuously removes ions without the need for chemical regeneration, enhancing efficiency. </p><p>3. **Ion Exchange**: This method swaps undesirable ions in water with more favorable ones, effectively reducing total dissolved solids and improving water purity. Each technology serves specific industrial applications, catering to diverse purity requirements.</p></p>
