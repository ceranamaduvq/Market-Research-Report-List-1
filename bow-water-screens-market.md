The market for bow screens can be divided into two main types: static bow screens and vibrating bow screens. Static bow screens utilize a fixed design, relying on gravity for water flow, while vibrating bow screens incorporate mechanical vibrations to enhance particle separation and throughput. Both types effectively remove debris and sediments, catering to distinct operational requirements and industry standards.</p></p>
