<p><p>The high-purity epoxy resin market is primarily segmented into three types: Bisphenol A epoxy resin, Bisphenol F epoxy resin, and others. Bisphenol A epoxy resin is widely used for its excellent mechanical properties and adhesion qualities, making it suitable for coatings and adhesives. Bisphenol F epoxy resin offers improved chemical resistance and lower viscosity, ideal for applications requiring high-temperature resistance. The "others" category includes specialty resins that cater to niche markets, such as flexible or modified epoxies, fulfilling specific performance needs.</p></p>
