<p><p>The metallized plastic film capacitor market is primarily divided into AC and DC film capacitors. AC film capacitors are designed for alternating current applications, typically used in power electronics, motor runs, and power factor correction. They handle higher voltage variations efficiently. DC film capacitors, on the other hand, are optimized for direct current applications, commonly found in power supplies and energy storage systems. Both types leverage metallized films for enhanced electrical performance, reliability, and compactness, catering to diverse electronic circuit demands.</p></p>
