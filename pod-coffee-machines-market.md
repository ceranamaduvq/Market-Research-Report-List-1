<p><p>Pod coffee machines are categorized into two main market types: closed source systems and open source systems. Closed source systems are proprietary models that only accept specific branded coffee pods, ensuring convenience but limiting consumer choice. In contrast, open source systems allow compatibility with various brands and types of pods, offering greater flexibility and cost savings. This diversity caters to different consumer preferences, with each type appealing to distinct market segments based on convenience, variety, and affordability.</p></p>
