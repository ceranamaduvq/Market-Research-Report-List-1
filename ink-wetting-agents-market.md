<p><p>Ink wetting agents are categorized into two main types: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based wetting agents are predominantly used in eco-friendly inks, offering superior interfacial tension reduction and facilitating smooth ink flow on various substrates. In contrast, solvent-based wetting agents are employed in traditional ink formulations, effective in enhancing dispersion and adhesion properties. Each type serves distinct applications, catering to the specific needs of printing processes while improving ink performance and overall print quality.</p></p>
