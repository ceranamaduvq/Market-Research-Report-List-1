<p><p>The hand juicers market features two main types: the vertical auger model and the horizontal auger model. The vertical auger model operates by pressing fruits downwards through a vertical chute, maximizing juice extraction with minimal oxidation, making it ideal for leafy greens. In contrast, the horizontal auger model processes ingredients horizontally, allowing for a higher yield from fibrous fruits and vegetables. Both models are designed for efficient juice extraction while being practical for home use, appealing to health-conscious consumers.</p></p>
